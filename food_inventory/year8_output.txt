 Week 9 : Time for a quick Dinner  
Beef Nachos (per 
group of 4)  
 
100g beef mince  
1 Brown onion diced  
1 T minced garlic   
¼ can of chilli beans  
2 T Tomato paste  
½ can chopped tomatoes  
½ cup frozen corn  
1 carrot grated  
½  c beef stock ( 1 beef stock cube + ½ c water)  
½ t smoked paprika  
½ t dried oregano  
½ t onion powder  
½ t ground cumin  
½ t ground coriander  
 
To serve  
 
½ c cheese  
Sour cream  
Corn chips  
 
Method  
 
1. Heat a little oil in a large frying pan on high heat cook mince, garlic and onion 
breaking up mince with a wooden spoon as it cooks until browned add spices.  
 
2. Stir in beans, to mato paste, tomatoes, corn, and carrot and beef stock  
 
 
3. Bring to a simmer then reduce heat to low stirring occasionally for 9 -12 
minutes until thickened.  
 
4. Put corn chips in a bowl and spoon mince over the top, sprinkle with cheese 
and put under grill until  till cheese is melted  
 
 
5. Top with sour cream
